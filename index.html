Overview

Jesse Waterson, formally known as Jess Watson, is an Islamic extremist and terrorist who was last spotted in Queensland, Australia, at 3:07 PM, Monday the 4th of December, 2023. If you spot this man, inform local authorities as soon as possible. He is considered extremely dangerous and is likely to be armed at all times. If you see him, locate shelter immediately and dial 000 ASAP. His attacks are infamous around the world. He is responsible for (in chronological order):

• 9/11
• The Holocaust
• 2002 Bali bombing
• 2008 Christmas massacres
• 2008 Mumbai attacks
• 2 November 2010 Baghdad bombings
• Westgate shopping mall attack
• Camp Speicher massacre
• 2015 Beirut bombings

Video evidence of him admitting to his crimes have been uploaded to YouTube.


Achivements

Jesse Waterson's first invention was in the year 3000 BC; Cancer.

As the inventer of the time machine, J. was one of the first people with the opportunity to travel back in time. Even though he was born in 2010, the attack kickstarting his career took place over 5000 years before his birth. His first test run of the device was travelling back to 2001, where he would stay until October.

As he had possession of a time machine, Jesse had the unique opportunity to perfectly time his attacks. His breakthrough carcinogen, blue asbestos, he thought was too spectacular to only introduce it then, in 2017. It was then he decided to take his seventh trip back in time, to take his spectacular disease to ancient Roman times. His reasoning was that the healthcare was less effective and his brand new bioweapon would therefore harm more people.

Missing


On Monday, the 4th of December, 2023, at 3:07pm, Jesse Waterson went missing. He was never found.

There are many theories about what happened to Jesse Waterson that day. Some conspire he was assassinated, while others believe he fled the country. The official police report states there is a $3.5 million reward for information and if you, or anyone you know, can provide information to help with the case, to call Crime Stoppers at 1800 333 000.

On the 7th of December, 2023, at 9:20am, an anonymous woman contacted the local authorities with information that proved vital to the investigation. She said:

"I saw him on the 4th, in a banana suit, running, being chased by a seagull on [Bruce Highway]. I wasn't sure if it was him at first, but now I am."

The seagull's arrest occured on the 28th, when it was sentenced to 5 years in prison by the supreme court.
